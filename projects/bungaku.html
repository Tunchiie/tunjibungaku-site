Billboard Music Analysis Platform - Active Development

View on GitHub

Project Overview

Technical Achievements & Business Impact

  • Analyzed 130K+ Billboard tracks spanning multiple decades to identify seasonal music trends and decade-based shifts
  • Implemented 13 statistical tests including ANOVA, Tukey HSD, and t-tests for robust pattern validation
  • Achieved 95% accuracy in predicting seasonal music preferences using statistical modeling
  • Discovered significant correlations between song characteristics and chart performance across different time periods
  • Built comprehensive data pipeline for processing and analyzing large-scale music industry datasets
  • Created interactive visualizations using Seaborn and Matplotlib for stakeholder presentations

Technical Implementation

Technology Stack

Python Pandas NumPy SciPy Seaborn Matplotlib Statistical Testing Data Visualization

Statistical Methods Applied

  • ANOVA (Analysis of Variance): Tested for significant differences in song characteristics across seasons
  • Tukey HSD Test: Post-hoc analysis to identify which specific seasons differed significantly
  • Independent t-tests: Compared song features between different decades and time periods
  • Correlation Analysis: Identified relationships between musical features and chart performance
  • Descriptive Statistics: Comprehensive summary statistics for all musical attributes
  • Trend Analysis: Time-series analysis to identify long-term patterns in music evolution

Key Findings & Insights

Seasonal Music Patterns

  • Summer tracks show significantly higher energy levels and danceability scores
  • Winter releases tend to have more acoustic elements and slower tempos
  • Holiday seasons correlate with increased use of traditional instruments
  • Spring releases demonstrate higher valence (musical positivity) ratings

Decade-Based Evolution

  • Significant increase in electronic elements from 1990s to 2010s
  • Gradual decrease in song duration over the analyzed time period
  • Rising prominence of hip-hop and pop fusion genres
  • Shift towards higher tempo and energy in mainstream music
