Lending Club Risk Model

Credit Risk Assessment System

XGBoost model processing 2.2M+ loan records with SHAP explainability for transparent risk assessment decisions. Predicts loan default probability with 77% accuracy and 0.76 F1-score.

View on GitHub

Project Overview

Technical Achievements & Business Impact

  • Processed 2.2M+ loan records from Lending Club to build comprehensive credit risk assessment model
  • Achieved 77% accuracy in predicting loan defaults using XGBoost with advanced feature engineering
  • Implemented SHAP explainability framework for transparent and auditable risk decisions
  • Optimized model performance with 0.76 F1-score balancing precision and recall for business needs
  • Developed robust data preprocessing pipeline handling missing values, outliers, and categorical encoding
  • Created comprehensive risk scoring system with interpretable feature importance rankings

Technical Implementation

Technology Stack

Python XGBoost SHAP Scikit-learn Pandas NumPy Risk Modeling Feature Engineering

Model Development Pipeline

  • Data Preprocessing: Comprehensive cleaning of 2.2M+ loan records with missing value imputation
  • Feature Engineering: Created debt-to-income ratios, credit utilization metrics, and temporal features
  • XGBoost Implementation: Gradient boosting with hyperparameter optimization for maximum performance
  • Model Validation: Stratified cross-validation and temporal validation for robust performance estimation
  • SHAP Integration: Model explainability for individual predictions and global feature importance
  • Risk Calibration: Probability calibration for accurate risk score interpretation

Model Performance & Risk Factors

Performance Metrics

  • 77% overall accuracy in default prediction across diverse loan portfolios
  • 0.76 F1-score demonstrating balanced precision and recall performance
  • AUC-ROC of 0.82 indicating strong discriminative ability between good and bad loans
  • Precision of 0.74 minimizing false positive risk assessments

Key Risk Indicators

  • Debt-to-income ratio emerges as strongest predictor of default risk
  • Credit score and credit history length show significant negative correlation with default
  • Loan purpose and employment length provide additional predictive power
  • Geographic and temporal factors reveal regional and seasonal risk patterns

Business Applications

Risk Management Applications

  • Automated loan approval/rejection decisions with confidence scoring
  • Dynamic interest rate pricing based on individual risk profiles
  • Portfolio risk assessment and diversification strategies
  • Early warning system for potential defaults in existing loan portfolio

Regulatory Compliance

  • SHAP explanations provide audit trail for regulatory compliance
  • Transparent feature importance rankings support fair lending practices
  • Model interpretability enables regulatory review and validation
  • Bias detection and mitigation through explainable AI techniques
